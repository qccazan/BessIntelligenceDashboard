# US-00-01 · Past-Year Historical Data Generation

## User Story

**As a** developer seeding the PoC database, **I want to** generate synthetic data covering a continuous rolling window of the past 12 months for all six datasets (D-01 through D-06), **so that** the Intelligence Layer has sufficient historical depth to support trend analysis, performance benchmarking, and advisory report generation.

## Description

For each dataset defined in the mock data specification, the system must generate synthetic data covering a continuous rolling window of the past 12 months (365 days), ending at the current date. All historical records must be internally consistent with the cross-dataset consistency rules. Time series data must use UTC-offset timestamps (Europe/Amsterdam, CET/CEST) and must not contain gaps, duplicate timestamps, or out-of-order entries.

Per-dataset volume requirements:
- D-01 Battery Master Data: Static per asset; commissioning dates within the past year where applicable.
- D-02 Real-Time Telemetry: One snapshot per hour for the past year as an archive; live snapshot is the most recent reading.
- D-03 Battery Historical Time Series: 96 intervals/day × 365 days = 35,040 records per asset.
- D-04 Day-Ahead Market Prices: 24 prices/day × 365 days = 8,760 hourly price records.
- D-05 AI Recommendation: 365 daily recommendation snapshots per asset group.
- D-06 Weather Data: 8,760 hourly readings per site × 12 sites = 105,120 records.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **AC-1**: Synthetic data for all six datasets (D-01 through D-06) covers a continuous rolling window of the past 12 months (365 days), ending at the current date.
- **AC-2**: D-01 commissioning dates fall within the past year where applicable; decommissioned assets appear in historical records but are absent from current snapshots.
- **AC-3**: D-02 provides one snapshot per hour for the past year as an archive; the live snapshot represents the most recent reading.
- **AC-4**: D-03 contains 35,040 records per asset (96 intervals/day × 365 days) at 15-minute resolution.
- **AC-5**: D-04 contains 8,760 hourly price records covering the past year of EPEX SPOT NL data.
- **AC-6**: D-05 contains 365 daily recommendation snapshots per asset group with charge/discharge windows and confidence scores.
- **AC-7**: D-06 contains 105,120 records (8,760 hourly readings × 12 sites) for the past year.
- **AC-8**: All time series data uses UTC-offset timestamps (Europe/Amsterdam, CET/CEST).
- **AC-9**: No time series contains gaps, duplicate timestamps, or out-of-order entries.
- **AC-10**: All historical records are internally consistent with the cross-dataset consistency rules defined in US-00-08.
