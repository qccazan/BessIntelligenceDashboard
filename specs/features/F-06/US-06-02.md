# US-06-02 · Battery State Assessment

## User Story

**As a** dispatch engine evaluating fleet readiness, **I want to** assess each asset's current SoC, SoH, and temperature against safety bounds and historical patterns, and classify the dispatch scenario based on weather conditions, **so that** only safe, dispatchable energy quantities are considered for the optimisation.

## Description

The engine reads current SoC, SoH, and temperature per asset from D-02, applies LFP chemistry safety bounds (charge ceiling 90%, discharge floor 15%, emergency hold < 10%, preferred band 20–80%), computes available-to-charge and dispatchable energy bounded by power rating × window duration × efficiency (η = 0.95), evaluates historical patterns from D-03b (7-day DoD baseline, time-at-high-SoC), classifies the weather dispatch scenario (Wind+Solar, Wind-only, Solar-only, Flat/Hold), and computes the Renewable Richness Score (RGS) per hour.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **AC-1**: The engine reads SoC, SoH, mode, and temperature from D-02 for all 12 assets.
- **AC-2**: LFP safety bounds are enforced: charge ceiling 90%, discharge floor 15%, emergency hold if SoC < 10%.
- **AC-3**: Available-to-charge energy = (90 − soc_pct) / 100 × capacity_kwh for each asset.
- **AC-4**: Dispatchable energy = (soc_pct − 15) / 100 × capacity_kwh for each asset.
- **AC-5**: Actual deliverable energy is bounded by min(available/dispatchable, power_rating_kw × window_hours × 0.95).
- **AC-6**: Average DoD over last 7 days is computed from D-03b (672 intervals, 7 × 96-interval days).
- **AC-7**: Time above 80% SoC in prior 24h is computed from D-03a as count(soc_pct > 80) / 96.
- **AC-8**: The dispatch scenario is classified as Wind+Solar, Wind-only, Solar-only, or Flat/Hold based on wind_speed_ms and GHI thresholds.
- **AC-9**: The RGS (Renewable Richness Score) is computed per hour with appropriate wind/solar weighting for overnight, solar, and transition hours.
