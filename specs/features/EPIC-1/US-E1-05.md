# US-E1-05 · Day-Ahead Market Prices (D-04)

## User Story

**As a** developer building the PoC data layer, **I want to** provide hourly day-ahead electricity prices for the next 24 hours from EPEX SPOT NL, **so that** the Market Forecast card can render the price chart and the F-06 engine can process price signals.

## Description

Hourly electricity prices for the next 24 hours as published by EPEX SPOT for the Netherlands bidding zone. Stored in `market_forecast_prices.json`. Contains: market ("EPEX SPOT NL"), currency ("EUR"), generated_at timestamp, and an array of 24 hourly price objects with hour_start and price_eur_mwh.

Realistic April weekday shape: evening peak ~17:00–19:00 (150–170 €/MWh), overnight trough ~02:00–05:00 (15–25 €/MWh), midday solar dip ~11:00–14:00 (55–80 €/MWh). Spread 7–10×.

## Acceptance Criteria

- **AC-1**: market = "EPEX SPOT NL", currency = "EUR".
- **AC-2**: prices array contains exactly 24 hourly objects.
- **AC-3**: Each price object has hour_start and price_eur_mwh.
- **AC-4**: Hours are spaced exactly 1 hour apart with no gaps.
- **AC-5**: Price curve includes evening peak ~150–170 €/MWh around 17:00–19:00.
- **AC-6**: Price curve includes overnight trough ~15–25 €/MWh around 02:00–05:00.
- **AC-7**: Price curve includes midday solar dip ~55–80 €/MWh around 11:00–14:00.
- **AC-8**: Spread between lowest and highest price is 7–10×.
- **AC-9**: All prices are positive numbers.
