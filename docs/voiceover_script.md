# BESS Intelligence — Mobile App Voiceover Script

> **Total duration:** ~62 seconds
> **Tone:** Warm, confident, pitch-style narration
> **Note:** Describes functionality without citing specific data values

---

## Scene 1 — Login (0:00 – 0:06)

"Meet BESS Intelligence — an AI-augmented layer that sits right on top of your existing battery monitoring platform. Getting started takes just seconds: sign in, and you're in."

## Scene 2 — Portfolio Dashboard (0:06 – 0:15)

"Your entire portfolio loads at a glance. Battery storage capacity, real-time availability, net power flow, and even your solar panel production — all synced live, all in one place."

## Scene 3 — Fleet Overview (0:15 – 0:30)

"Scroll through your full fleet of assets, organized by site. Each battery shows its current state, capacity, and health status — so you can instantly spot which units are idle, charging, discharging, or flagged with a fault."

## Scene 4 — Battery Detail & Current State (0:30 – 0:42)

"Tap any battery to dive deeper. You'll see live state of charge, state of health, temperature, and power rating — everything you need to assess a single unit at a glance."

## Scene 5 — Yesterday Replay (0:42 – 0:51)

"Now here's where it gets interesting. The replay feature lets you scrub through yesterday's full activity — watching charge and discharge cycles unfold in real time, with running totals that track energy in, energy out, and net cycles."

## Scene 6 — AI Recommendation Forecast (0:51 – 1:02)

"And this is the intelligence layer. Our AI analyzes day-ahead market prices and generates a coordinated charge-discharge strategy across your entire portfolio — showing you exactly when to buy low and sell high, with a confidence score and estimated revenue per cycle. Every recommendation comes with clear, explainable reasoning and per-battery action plans."
