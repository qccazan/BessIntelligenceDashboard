# BESS Intelligence Dashboard

We built this dashboard to help Battery Energy Storage companies act on the opportunities provided by the energy market — and not rely solely on solar panels to charge their batteries.

Energy markets are volatile. Prices spike, prices crash — sometimes within the same hour. For companies that own Battery Energy Storage Systems, that volatility isn't a problem. It's an opportunity.

The challenge is: how do you consistently act on that opportunity, at the right moment?

This means companies can generate arbitrage revenue, reduce electricity costs, and protect their battery investment — all without needing to be energy market experts. Everything lives in one place, and decisions that used to require constant manual oversight now happen with clarity and confidence.

The result: energy storage stops being a passive asset and becomes an active source of revenue.
